## About the Group

Group Cao is led by **Yufei Cao**, Associate Professor and PhD Supervisor at the **School of Food Science and Engineering, South China University of Technology**. Our research bridges **biocatalysis, chemical catalysis, electrocatalysis, computational biology, computational chemistry, food analysis, and data-driven discovery**.

We are particularly interested in using catalytic science and computation to expand the boundaries of enzyme function, understand catalytic mechanisms across scales, and develop practical systems for synthesis and food-related applications.

Professor Cao has published **more than 20 SCI papers** in journals including **Nature Catalysis, Nature Communications, Trends in Biotechnology, JACS Au, and ACS Nano**.

## Research Themes

### 1. Enzyme–Chemical Hybrid Catalysis

We combine the strengths of enzymatic catalysis and chemical catalysis to construct integrated catalytic systems for cascade and cooperative transformations, aiming to expand the functional scope of biocatalysis.

### 2. Rational Enzyme Design

We use **AI, molecular dynamics simulations, QM/MM, and related computational tools** to design and engineer enzymes with broader substrate scopes and new-to-nature catalytic capabilities.

### 3. Enzyme Catalysis in Confined Spaces

We study how confinement, interfaces, and local microenvironments regulate catalytic activity, selectivity, and stability, with the goal of establishing mechanism-guided design principles.

### 4. Food Detection and Food Big Data Analytics

We develop methods for food analysis, intelligent detection, and large-scale data mining to support food quality, safety, and functional evaluation.
