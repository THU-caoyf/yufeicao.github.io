Group Cao welcomes motivated researchers who are interested in catalysis, enzyme engineering, computation, and interdisciplinary food science.

## Postdoctoral Researchers

We welcome applicants with backgrounds in catalysis, biocatalysis, computational chemistry, molecular simulation, food science, materials, chemical engineering, or related areas.

Please email the following materials to the PI:

- cover letter
- curriculum vitae
- representative publications or preprints
- brief summary of past research and future interests

## PhD and Master’s Students

Students with strong interests in catalysis, molecular design, computation, food chemistry, or data science are encouraged to contact us.

When reaching out, please include:

- CV
- transcripts if available
- short statement of research interest
- any prior research experience

## Undergraduate Researchers

Undergraduate students are welcome to join group meetings and participate in research projects. Students with interests in experiments, coding, modeling, or data analysis are especially encouraged to apply.

## Research Environment

Our group values curiosity, rigor, collaboration, and interdisciplinary thinking. We encourage students to integrate experiments with theory and computation.
